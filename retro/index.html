Take a Step Back in Time

New Game Plus isn't just a retro video game store. It is a time capsule. A window to a simpler time. A celebration of the amazing memories we all made playing the games that would define a generation. Here at New Game Plus, we strive to recreate the atmosphere and comfort these retro games provided us and in doing so, build a community where we can share our love and passion for these games together. Whether you are an old or young gamer, there is something for you here. We offer a large variety of past and present video games as well as an arcade. Everyone is welcome and we would love to personally invite you to be a part of our gaming community here at New Game Plus. We look forward to seeing you here!
